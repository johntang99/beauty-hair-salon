## üí° **Custom Theme Creation Tips**

### **Primary Color Selection:**
- Choose based on industry standards
- Consider cultural meanings
- Test for accessibility (WCAG contrast)
- Ensure it matches brand personality

### **Secondary Color Selection:**
- Complement the primary (color wheel)
- Provide visual variety
- Use for accents, not overwhelming
- Consider warm vs cool balance

### **Backdrop Colors:**
- Use very light tints (50 shades)
- Subtle gradients work best
- Ensure text readability
- Keep it professional
